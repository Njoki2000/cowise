CO-WISE

Consortium for Women in STEM

Co-wise is a consortium that seeks to empower kids from the slum areas to embrace STEM-related courses i.e Sciences,Technology,Engineering and Mathematics.

Our Vision

To foster a STEM-driven generation

Our Mission

To spark curiosity, nuture imagination, and enable the upcoming generation through engaging STEM encounters.

Core-Values

  • Innovation
  • Inclusivity
  • Collaboration
  • Empowerment
